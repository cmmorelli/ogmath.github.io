Defining Symbols

Click here to see notes When reading from left to right, we identify the following inequality symbols:

  • \(<\) means "less than" (open circle,
    arrow to the left)
  • \(>\) means "greater than" (open circle,
    arrow to the right)
  • \(\leq\) means "less than or equal to"
    (closed circle, arrow to the left)
  • \(\geq\) means "greater than or equal to"
    (closed circle, arrow to the right)
