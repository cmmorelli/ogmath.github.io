Polynomial

  • Poly means many.
  • Nomial means term.
  • ∴ Polynomial means many terms.

Monomial

  • Mono means one.
  • Nomial means term.
  • ∴ Monomial means one term.

Binomial

  • Bi means two.
  • Nomial means term.
  • ∴ Binomial means two terms.

Trinomial

  • Tri means three.
  • Nomial means term.
  • ∴ Trinomial means three terms.

Polynomials with More Than Three Terms

  • All polynomials with more than three terms is named according to the degree of the polynomial.
  • The degree of a polynomial is equal to the highest degree of any term in the polynomial.
