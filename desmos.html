Desmos Linear Regression Equation

What You Will Learn

  • Learn how to find, slope, the y-intercept, and the slope-intercept form equation of a line (\(y=mx+b\)) using the Desmos Linear Regression Equation.
  • Here is a full YouTube tutorial video: Desmos Linear Regression Tutorial

Basic Entry

  • First, go to Desmos, click Launch App, click “Choose Assessment,” type “Texas” and select “Texas (STAAR)”, ensure the Algebra 1 graphing calculator is selected and then click on “Start Practice.”
  • Once in Desmos Test Mode, click the plus sign + and then click the “Table” option.
  • Enter at least two coordinate pairs.
  • Type this equation on a new link: y 1 ~ m x 1 + b
  • The m value is the slope. The b value is the y-intercept.

How Desmos Shows Undefined & Zero Slopes

  • If the line is not passing through both points then the slope is undefined.
  • If you see an m value of \(1.7764 \times 10^{-16}\) that means the slope is zero.
